The Hackathon Challenge

Challenge

Assignments

  • Assignment 1: Results of the brainstorming

    Deadline: Friday 16 April 2021, 11:00

    Share your results of the brainstorming with us. Describe your idea or ideas. What are the added values of your product/service? What would you like to improve with your concept?

    Length: 50 - 100 words.

    Please fill out the form below:

  • Assignment 2: Progress report - results of the first day

    Deadline: Friday 16 April 2021, 16:00

    Summarise your results from the first day. Create one or two PowerPoint slide and record your results. You can use this slide for your final presentation tomorrow. Save the PowerPoint as a PDF file and upload it in the form below.

  • Submission of the final presentation

    Deadline: Saturday, 17 April 2021 at 12:00 noon.

    Allowed is a PowerPoint presentation with 3 content slides and a title page containing the name of your mini-enterprise and school.

    Save your presentation as a PDF document. Name the file with the name of your mini-enterprise. (Example: The Crunchy Company â†’ "the_crunchy_company.pdf")

    Upload your presentation using the forms below:

Evaluation criteria

  • The content of the concept paper (30 %)
    • PowerPoint presentation with 3 content slides and 1 title slide (with the name of the mini-enterprise and the school).
    • Concept of the product / service (sketch/visualisation, USP)
    • Marketing mix (4Ps and their alignment)
    • A consulting-style presentation is expected. The slides should comprehensively and self-explanatory cover the concept.
  • Innovation and creativity (30 %)
  • Quality of the presentation (40 %)
    • 5min presentation (at least 2 people presenting)
    • 5min Q&A (entire team participates)
    • Same content as concept paper: Concept of the product / service (sketch/visualisation, USP, competitor analysis)
